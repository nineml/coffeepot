CoffeePot

Introduction

The coffeepot application is a small wrapper around the underlying CoffeeFilter library which is, in turn, a wrapper around the underlying CoffeeGrinder parser.

  • CoffeePot is the command line interface.

  • CoffeeFilter is the Invisible XML library.

  • CoffeeGrinder is the Earley parser.

CoffeePot marshals the grammar and inputs specified into requests against the underlying libraries.
